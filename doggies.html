Indian Pariah Dog

The caring & fostering of street dogs is crucial as the roads being their only home has
made them face a lot of hardship . These helpless creatures fight for their lives daily ,
rescuing them from such cruelties & bringing smiles on these faces is the kindest act of all .
Join us in our journey of providing them a place to call home .

Service Dogs

A dog that could be your support system when you are in need . Service dogs who
either retired or didn't make it through the program are well trained & qualified .
A person with a disability could apply to this & if he/she is selected then they goes
through training before recieving the dog. 

Special needs Dog

Special needs dogs are fully adoptable dogs that just need a little extra due to
physical disabilities, such as blindness, deafness, or amputation. Many of these dogs
have some of the biggest and best personalities . Just with your extra efforts & love ,
they could find their forever home-you.
